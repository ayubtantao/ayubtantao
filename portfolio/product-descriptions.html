Three different tones

Each product has a different buyer and requires a different voice — technical for the gadget, gentle and sensory for skincare, warm and practical for home goods. Tone-matching is a key writing skill this sample demonstrates.

Features vs benefits

Good product copy leads with what the buyer feels or gains, not just specs. Notice how each description leads with the outcome before listing technical details.

Scannable format

Name, tagline, paragraph description, then feature bullets. This mirrors the format used by Shopify and WooCommerce product pages — ready to paste, no reformatting needed.

Three descriptions across different categories and tones. Each is written to match the product's buyer — not just list features.

Sample 01 — Tech / Gadget
NovaSpark Wireless Charging Pad
Full charge. Zero cables. Finally.

The NovaSpark is a 15W fast-charging pad built for people who are done untangling cables. Set your phone down — that's all there is to it. The non-slip surface keeps your device in place, the LED indicator confirms charging has started, and the slim profile fits anywhere on your desk without taking over it. Compatible with all Qi-enabled devices including iPhone 12 and above, Samsung Galaxy S series, and AirPods.

  • 15W fast charging
  • Universal Qi compatibility
  • Non-slip surface
  • LED charging indicator
  • No cable tangle
Sample 02 — Skincare / Beauty
Lumière Daily Hydration Serum
Lightweight. Lasting. Actually works.

Some serums feel heavy and take forever to absorb. Lumière doesn't. This lightweight formula sinks into skin in seconds, delivering 72-hour moisture without the greasy finish. Formulated with hyaluronic acid and niacinamide, it works equally well under makeup or on its own. Fragrance-free and dermatologist-tested — suitable for sensitive, combination, and oily skin types. Results visible in as little as two weeks of daily use.

  • 72-hour hydration
  • Hyaluronic acid + niacinamide
  • Fragrance-free
  • Dermatologist tested
  • All skin types
Sample 03 — Home Goods
Fernwood Ceramic Pour-Over Set
Better coffee. Simpler mornings.

The Fernwood pour-over set strips coffee-making back to what matters — good water, good beans, and the right vessel. The hand-thrown ceramic dripper retains heat better than glass or plastic, and the matte finish means it won't show fingerprints or water marks. Comes with a reusable stainless steel filter, so no paper filters to run out of. Dishwasher safe. Holds enough for two generous cups. The kind of thing you use every day and never get tired of looking at.

  • Hand-thrown ceramic
  • Reusable steel filter
  • Dishwasher safe
  • 2-cup capacity
  • Matte finish
