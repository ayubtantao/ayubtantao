Six different tones

Promotional, educational, and engagement-focused captions — each requires a different energy. Promotional drives action, educational builds trust, engagement starts conversation.

Platform awareness

Facebook captions can be longer and more conversational. Instagram rewards punchy openers and strategic hashtags. These samples reflect those differences.

The opener matters most

On social media, the first line is what shows before the "see more" cutoff. Every caption here opens with a hook — a question, a bold statement, or something that creates curiosity.

Educational Captions
FacebookEducational — Tips Format
Most people apply serum wrong. Here's the right way: ✔ Apply to slightly damp skin — not dry. Damp skin absorbs actives better. ✔ Use 3-4 drops. More isn't more effective. ✔ Pat, don't rub. Patting helps absorption without dragging the skin. ✔ Wait 60 seconds before applying moisturizer. Small habits, visible difference. Save this for your next skincare routine.
